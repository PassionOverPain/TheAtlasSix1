The Atlas Six

The Legend tells a tale of Six heroes

In a world teetering on the brink of apocalypse, darkness shattered the light and unleashed unknown horrors upon humanity. Amidst the chaos, hope lay dormant in the form of six extraordinary individuals the Atlas Six scattered across a fractured world. Each possessed unique abilities and destinies entwined with the fate of humanity. As they navigated through the ruins of civilization, their paths converged, forging an uneasy alliance against both the monstrous creatures prowling the land and their own inner demons. Bound by a shared mission and the flickering ember of hope, the Atlas Six faced a journey that would test their resilience, challenge their alliances, and unravel mysteries buried deep within time itself. Their quest held the key to either defying the apocalypse or succumbing to its relentless advance.

Born amidst the serene beauty of Aurelia—a kingdom of lush forests and glistening lakes—Jasmin has always been deeply connected to nature. From her earliest years, she showed a rare affinity for animals, able to sense their emotions and calm even the most wary with just a touch. Her family, belonging to a lineage of Druids gifted in taming and bonding with creatures, saw her potential and nurtured her talents. Alongside her training in Druidic arts and water magic, Jasmin mastered the healing arts, becoming an adept healer. Today, as the healer of the Atlas Six, her closest friends, she brings a gentle strength to their ranks, often guided by her loyal subjects, her closest companions and protectors: Bleeky and Jack.

Llewellyn stands as one of the towering generals of Eryndor, the stone-wrought kingdom famed for its fierce warriors and steadfast titans. Hailing from humble roots, Llewellyn rose through the ranks of Eryndor’s formidable military on sheer tenacity, quickly gaining renown for his skill in combat and his unbreakable will. He weilds a massive sword and shield forged from the indomitable stones of his homeland. Llewellyn is the embodiment of a mountain—grounded, calm, and unwaveringly loyal. His demeanor is steady and measured, offering a quiet strength that becomes a beacon of hope for those around him. His reputation as the tank of the Atlas Six is well-earned: when battle calls, he takes on the fiercest of foes, using his shield to deflect, his blade to conquer, and his very presence to intimidate.

Mthi hails from Valdora, the land of sorcery, archery, and boundless knowledge, where her upbringing was steeped in both discipline and curiosity. Known as "The Eye" for her unmatched precision in archery, she earned her title not just for her aim but for her uncanny ability to notice what others miss. Raised in a family deeply respected in Valdora, Mthi’s love for books and learning began at an early age, often exploring Valdora’s labyrinthine library of ancient tomes and scrolls. Her inquisitiveness has made her surprisingly knowledgeable, with insights into topics ranging from enchantments to crafting techniques. Beyond her studies and sharp aim, Mthi has a deep passion for music. Whether strumming a melody on her guitar or composing intricate harmonies, she finds joy in the connection music brings. Her dream of one day forming a band reflects her playful spirit, though her skills as a bard already make her invaluable to her companions. With a personality as vibrant as the melodies she plays, Mthi’s balance of intellect, artistry, and precision makes her a vital and captivating presence in any group.

Zanele, the fiery spirit of the Atlas Six, was born and raised in Kurohana, a harsh but beautiful land where volcanoes spew molten fury and the earth itself is forged in flame. From an early age, she was trained in the destructive arts of fire magic, learning to harness its volatile power. Yet, it was under the guidance of her grandmother that she discovered the healing side of flame, learning to use her fire not only to burn but to restore and mend. With a heart as fierce as her magic, Zanele became the motherly figure of the group, ever protective and nurturing. Her presence is a constant, her warmth extending beyond the battlefield, offering solace to those who need it most. Whether fighting with unrelenting force or offering care with a gentle touch, Zanele’s devotion to the Atlas Six knows no bounds.

Time is the gift that robs you of the present. Tino is a mysterious figure with the ability to control time, shrouded in riddles and paradoxes. His words often carry double meanings, blending philosophy with playful ambiguity. His eyes, always looking beyond the present, suggest he's aware of things you’re not. He asks existential questions, like if the past is truly gone or just waiting to be revisited, and whether the future is fixed or malleable. His charm lies in making you ponder deep concepts while keeping a playful grin, as though time itself is just another game for him.
